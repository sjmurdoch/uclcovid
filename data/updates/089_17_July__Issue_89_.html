3. Changes to the recruitment freeze

A recruitment freeze has been in place since March 2020 which has helped our finances.


In the light of our latest student number projections, we can start a phased and controlled relaxation of the recruitment freeze for some academic and professional services posts in Schools where student numbers look likely to exceed previous forecasts.


It is important to reiterate that the recruitment freeze remains in place. Departments and Faculties must have explored all reasonable options for sourcing cover internally, including cover from other Faculties/Departments/Divisions and furlough staff, before making a request.


Please see the Finance and Business Affairs website for an overview of the exception criteria and process for permanent and temporary recruitment during the freeze.
