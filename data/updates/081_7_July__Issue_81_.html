2. Making savings by reducing UCL's non-pay expenditure

The Procurement team have been working across the university to find ways to cut back on our non-pay expenditure and to reduce complexity, so you can get things done more quickly and follow best practice.

4. Teams Tips Tuesday

Today is Teams Tips Tuesday, with hints and ideas from ISD. This week, we are going back to basics with some general tips around Teams etiquette.


Top Tip for meetings: always join a meeting with your camera off and microphone muted, particularly for large meetings. Muting the mic when you’re not speaking is also really helpful and means the focus can stay on whoever is talking.


The keyboard shortcut to mute/unmute yourself quickly is Ctrl + shift + M (Cmd on Mac). Find out more about scheduling and running Teams meetings on the Teams SharePoint site.


Top Tip for posting: keep a conversation focused by replying to the original comment (nesting the thread) and not starting a new post. If you need to compose a longer reply, click the ‘Format’ icon (the A with a paintbrush) that appears directly under the message box. This expands the box and allows for paragraphs and formatting. If you start a new post by mistake, you can delete it by clicking on the ellipsis (…) and selecting ‘delete’. Find out more about posting on the Teams SharePoint site.


If you have any tips for using Teams you want to share, please email isd-webcomms@ucl.ac.uk or post your tip on Twitter or Instagram with the hashtag #teamstipstuesday. For further information about using Teams, please have a look at the Teams SharePoint site. If you are having problems using Teams, please contact the IT Services Desk.

Your health and wellbeing

If you feel unwell and think that you may have coronavirus, please follow the latest Government advice. You can also find more information on the NHS website.

                                                

If you are worried about coronavirus or your wellbeing more generally, please speak to your line manager or check our list of resources to support staff during the outbreak.


You can access free counselling services via the Employee Assistance Programme, Care First, which is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Please telephone 0800 197 4510 to speak to an independent professional counsellor or information specialist in confidence.


Please advise any students who are worried to contact askUCL.
