2. Student accommodation

On the advice of Public Health England, we are relocating some students who are staying with us in UCL halls and are currently living in accommodation with shared bathrooms to halls with en-suite facilities. This will mean students can self-isolate safely if they become unwell and we reduce the risk of infection to other students, staff and our student residence advisers who are continuing to work in these halls. Of course, they will not be charged any extra fees and UCL will assist with all moving arrangements as instructed by Public Health England.


We are contacting all our students who will be affected directly. If you are approached by students who are worried about their living situation, please advise them to contact askUCL or their Hall Warden.
