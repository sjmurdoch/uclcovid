2. Teams Tips Tuesday

Today is Teams Tips Tuesday, with hints and ideas from ISD. This weekâ€™s tips focus on accessibility and eye comfort.

      

Top Tip: You can alter the look and feel of Teams by going to Settings > General. Here you can change the screen Theme from the default setting to Dark or High contrast. You can also alter your layout view from Grid to List or vice versa. 

      

If you are finding the buttons and text on Teams to be too small, you can adjust and zoom in at any time by pressing Ctrl + (or Cmd + on a Mac) and conversely shrink the size again by pressing Ctrl/Cmd -

      

If you have any tips for using Teams you want to share, please email isd-webcomms@ucl.ac.uk or post your tip on Twitter or Instagram with the hashtag #teamstipstuesday. For further information about using Teams effectively, please have a look at the Teams SharePoint site. If you are having problems with the set-up of Teams, please contact the IT Services Desk directly who are best placed to help you.
