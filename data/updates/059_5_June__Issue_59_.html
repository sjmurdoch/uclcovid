1. UCL's updated crisis management structure

The Gold / Silver / Bronze (GSB) structure allowed us to close down the majority of the campus and to undertake a huge amount of co-ordinated planning work for the delivery model in 2020-21.


We now need to focus on implementing plans and have moved away from the GSB structure towards a new framework. The focus of the new framework, starting from 8 June, continues to be set by the Provost and is supported by a Crisis Leadership Team. You can read more about our approach and the structure on the coronavirus information hub.


2. Temporary Operating Model for 2020-21

UCL will be open and ready to teach at the start of the new academic year under a new Temporary Operating Model. Importantly, it considers the enhanced capabilities needed to support the new ways of working as well and the support and training we will need to provide.


You can view the Temporary Operating Model on the coronavirus information hub.
