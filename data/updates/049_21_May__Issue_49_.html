3. The importance of Open Science / Open Scholarship

As the coronavirus pandemic continues, it is now more important than ever to collaborate and share the results of ongoing research and experimentation at every stage.


UCL has signed The Wellcome Statement on Open Data and issued its own statement to make clear our position on supporting research transparency in the context of the pandemic. We believe that the pandemic is a wake-up call to the global research community finally to adopt Open Science / Open Scholarship as a set of principles and values which should guide all future activity.


Please contact Paul Ayris, Pro-Vice-Provost (UCL Library Services), if you require any further information on this or on any other Open Science / Open Scholarship practices.
