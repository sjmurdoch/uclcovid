1. UCL to host COVID-19 testing centre


UCL’s Ramsay Hall will become a COVID-19 testing centre from today, as part of a partnership between the London Borough of Camden and the Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC). The new centre will increase the number of tests available to Camden residents, as part of UCL’s commitment to playing an active role in its community and supporting the local and national response to the pandemic.


You can find out more on UCL News.

                                                         

2. Supporting student travel for the winter break


On Friday we published a message to UCL students about travel and support over the winter break from Professor Anthony Smith and Carol Paige, Democracy, Operations and Community Officer for Students’ Union UCL.


This message has also been sent today to all students in the student newsletter, myUCL, and we will be sharing more information with students on Monday 23 November.


3. Your Wellbeing: Complete the survey for a chance to win £50


Please complete Week 22 of the ‘Your Wellbeing’ survey which is now live on the HR website – it takes less than five minutes and will help the Provost and the leadership team understand how staff are coping during the pandemic.

                  

HR are currently offering three £50 Amazon vouchers to winners who will be picked from members of staff who complete four consecutive Your Wellbeing surveys – find out more on the HR website.

                                                                   

4. Coronavirus: The Whole Story podcast – How is the pandemic affecting women differently?


This week, in part one of a two-part special, Vivienne Parry is joined by Dr Daisy Fancourt and Dr Tamjid Mujtaba, who discuss UCL research confirming that women have found the pandemic and lockdown more challenging than men.


If you have questions about Coronavirus that you would like our UCL experts to answer, please do let us know – submit your ideas here!
