3. How UCL staff and students are tackling coronavirus

UCL staff working in our laboratories are donating supplies of nitrile gloves to UCLH, which is in urgent need of Personal Protective Equipment for NHS workers treating coronavirus patients.

If you know of any departments with more unopened boxes of gloves, please drop them off at the North Lodge at the main entrance to UCL on Gower Street, as we are planning to make another delivery to UCLH next week.
